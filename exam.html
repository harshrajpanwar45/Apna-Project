About Our Simple Project

Our goal was to demonstrate how a multi-page feel can be created using only standard web technologies (HTML and CSS) without reliance on external libraries.

Our Technology Stack:

  • HTML: Structure and Content.
  • CSS: Basic colors, spacing, layout, and the crucial :target selector for navigation.
